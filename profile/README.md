### 🌐 Overview
StackDyno provides purpose-built modules for every aspect of Google Cloud cost management. We help resellers turn cost optimization into a competitive advantage through visibility, AI-driven support, and deep infrastructure insights.

### 🛠️ Core Capabilities
* **Reseller Command Center:** A multi-customer portfolio view to manage spend, savings opportunities, and alerts across all customers from a single dashboard.
* **Finn AI (FinOps Agent):** An AI-powered assistant that automates level 1 support by answering customer questions about billing, usage, and resources.
* **Cloud Map:** Interactive GCP infrastructure diagrams that overlay cost and utilization data directly onto your resource topology.
* **Whitelabel Ready:** Built for resellers to deploy under their own brand with custom logos, colors, and domains.

### ⚡ Technical Focus
Our engineering efforts center around deep integration with the Google Cloud ecosystem:
- **GCP Native:** Heavy utilization of BigQuery billing exports, GKE, GCS, and Google Cloud APIs.
- **Kubernetes (GKE):** Granular cost allocation and optimization for containerized workloads.
- **Automated Operations:** Anomaly alerting and scheduled reporting for Google Cloud environments.
- **Integrations:** Seamless workflows with Slack, PagerDuty, and Kubernetes.
